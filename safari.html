SAFARI UNDER THE STARS RAFFLE

WIN A SMOKY MOUNTAIN ESCAPE!

Travel to Bluff Mountain, Tennessee near Gatlinburg for an incredible getaway with family and friends at the Cabins of the Smoky Mountains, ranked number one family resort in the Smokies by Southern Living Magazine.

Enjoy a four-night stay in a luxurious four-bedroom cabin that sleeps between 8 and 14 people and is pet friendly. Enjoy breathtaking views of the Great Smoky Mountains. Do laps in the indoor swimming pool or relax in the sauna. Be entertained playing pool, shuffle board and watching movies in your very own theater room. Hike the mountain trails inside the national park. Go ziplining, whitewater rafting, skiing, play golf and so much more. Nearby is all of the shopping , dining and entertainment to dream of along the streets in downtown Gatlinburg. This trip offers something for everyone!

This incredible package includes:

  • Four nights at the Mountain Pool and Views cabin.
  • 8 tickets to Ripley’s Aquarium of the Smokies
  • $1,000 cash for miscellaneous expenses

Travel is at your discretion but will need to book in advance to ensure the dates you want are available. Blackout dates do apply*. The raffle winner will be drawn at Safari Under the Stars on Saturday, April 30, 2022, though need not be present to win. Tickets are $100 apiece, and proceeds support the Zoo’s animal wellness, conservation and education programs. Only 400 tickets will be sold.

Blackout dates are July 1-10, 2022; November 21-27, 2022; and December 19, 2022-January 8, 2023. Must book within one year from date of raffle drawing which is on or before April 30, 2023.
