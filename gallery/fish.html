Lepisosteus oculatus

Lepisosteus oculatus

Scientific name: Hystrix brachyurus
Order: Rodent (Rodentia)
Family: Hedgehog (Hystricidae)

Distribution: The porcupine is a common species found throughout the mountainous and midland regions of Vietnam. Also available in China, Malaysia, Indonesia.

Habitat: They live mainly in mountainous forests, mixed bamboo forests, shrub forests

Biology: Porcupines live in pairs or small families in burrows. A slow, timid animal. Nocturnal foraging activity.

Food: The food is completely plant-based, especially the hedgehog likes to eat: cassava and corn, ripe fruits that fall on the ground such as figs, figs, jasmine, vertical, bananas.
